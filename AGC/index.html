Please discover our glass range for Automotive applications.

For each family of products (Windshield, Rear Window, Side Windows, Sunroof), we invite you to select a specific Glass color (clear, green, grey) and transparency (light or dark). This application lets you view glass on the vehicle, from outside or from inside the car.

AGC Automotive Glass range offers optimum properties for light and energy transmission. We wish you a pleasant glass discovery trip.

  • LT: Light Transmission
  • LR: Light Reflection
  • ET: Energy Transmission
  • ER: Energy Reflection
  • EA: Energy Absorption
  • Solar Factor: Total energy transmittance transmitted through the glazing. Sum of the radiation transmitted directly and that which is absorbed and emitted back to inside.
  • SFO(TTs): Solar Factor at 0km/h
  • SF100: Solar Factor at 100km/h
  • Selectivity: ratio of Light Transmission and Solar Factor (LT/SF)
